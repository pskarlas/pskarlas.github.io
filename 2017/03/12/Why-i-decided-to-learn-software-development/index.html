Why i decided to learn Software Development

Panagiotis Skarlas
2017-03-12

Intro

I’ll start off by saying that Programming/Software development is a form of art. It is a highly creative process that blends in analytical and problem solving skills with a strong “no-quit” mindset.

Am i curious?

It all started when i was browsing web apps couple of years back and i was curious, asking myself “How can they do that?” or “How does that work?”. This curiosity led me to find out the ‘how’, deep dive and try to understand, and as you might already have guessed the only way i could accomplish that was through learning about software development. I began making baby steps by building small websites and thats when i understood that i take a really serious joy in programming! Why? Because i become a craftsman, an artist who can take hard logic and use it creatively to produce something unique and often intriguing.

Creativity matters!

Creativity, the root of Software Development, matters a lot to me, is what makes my life fun and is what gets me excited each and everyday when i wake up in the morning. Software development simply embraces creativity, it is truly a creative problem itself. So what more could i ask for? My only option was to get serious about software development and start the process of never-ending learning.
